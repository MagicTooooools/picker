# Zilvia.net Data Breach

## What Happened

In November 2025, data breached from [the Zilvia.net Nissan 240SX Silvia and Z Fairlady car forum](https://www.zilvia.net/) was leaked. The breach exposed 288k unique email addresses along with usernames, IP addresses and salted MD5 password hashes sourced from the vBulletin based platform. Attempts to contact Zilvia.net about the incident were unsuccessful.

Email addresses
