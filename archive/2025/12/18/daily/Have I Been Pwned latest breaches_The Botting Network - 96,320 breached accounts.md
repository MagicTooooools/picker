# The Botting Network Data Breach

## What Happened

In August 2012, the forum for making money with botting "The Botting Network" [suffered a data breach that exposed 96k user records](https://x.com/r00tbeer_/status/237213939803504641). The now defunct vBulletin forum leaked 96k email addresses, usernames, dates of birth and salted MD5 password hashes.

## Compromised Data

Dates of birth

Email addresses

Passwords

Usernames

## Recommended Actions

##### Change Your Password

If you haven’t already changed the password affected by this breach, do
so immediately on every account where it was used.

##### Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Wherever 2FA is supported, add an extra layer of security to your
account.
