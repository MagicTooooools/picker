# Features [â€‹](#features)

[![Features visualization](/images/personal-ai-maturity-v1.png)](/images/personal-ai-maturity-v1.png)

Features visualization

These are roughly in order of likely technical possibility. Also, there's still lots of room to improve in the ones that already happened.

1. : You ask a thing in text, and it can answer back.
2. : The system knows a decent amount about you, so it can customize its results based on its knowledge.
3. : This can always get better but became pretty solid in 2024.
4. **Zero Friction Access**: You can use your assistant when you're away from your primary interface, like your home computer, its terminal, or even a particular website.
5. **Continuous Activities**: Our assistant can continue working for arbitrary amounts of time while we're doing other things.
6. **Persistent Voice**: Any time, anywhere, you can just whisper or speak to have your assistant start working for you.
7. **Persistent Sight**: Your assistant can see what you see, and also behind you (as well as around you, if it has access to other cameras).
8. **Full Computer Use**: You can navigate with voice and gesture while your assistant does the work using your system.

Some of these may go in a different order and/or be worked on simultaneously, just based on how the tech unfolds.

I would say we are roughly at #4, which is precisely the feature that I'm working on next for the PAI project.
