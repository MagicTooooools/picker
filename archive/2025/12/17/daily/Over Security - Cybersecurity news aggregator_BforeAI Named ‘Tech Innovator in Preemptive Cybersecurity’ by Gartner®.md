## BforeAI Named ‘Tech Innovator in Preemptive Cybersecurity’ by Gartner®

![preemptive cybersecurity report image](https://bfore.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/preemptive-cybersecurity-report-image-2-1024x390.png)

BforeAI Named ‘Tech Innovator in Preemptive Cybersecurity’ by Gartner®

New York, New York – January 8, 2025

BforeAI, a pioneer in predictive attack intelligence, today announced its inclusion in the Gartner report, Emerging Tech: Tech Innovators in Preemptive Cybersecurity. We believe the recognition highlights BforeAI’s innovative approach to shifting cyber defense from a reactive to a preemptive posture.

The Gartner report identifies BforeAI for its Predictive Attack Intelligence service that “proactively alerts users to digital threats and orchestrates infrastructure takedowns.” This unique capability is delivered through the company’s PreCrime Intelligence solution, which uses a predictive AI system to generate a proprietary PreCrime score, enabling preemptive measures before an attack occurs.

According to Gartner, “By 2030, preemptive cybersecurity technologies will be included in 75% of security solutions that are currently focused solely on detection and response.” The report also notes that emerging GenAI-driven threats are challenging traditional detection and response strategies, necessitating a shift to preemptive solutions.
