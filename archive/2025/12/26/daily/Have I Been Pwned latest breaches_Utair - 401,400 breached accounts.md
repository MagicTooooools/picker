# Utair Data Breach

## What Happened

In August 2020, [news broke of a data breach of Russian airline Utair that dated back to the previous year](https://www.ruaviation.com/news/2020/8/26/15357/?h). The breach contained over 400k unique email addresses along with extensive personal information including names, physical addresses, dates of birth, passport numbers and loyalty program details.

Genders
