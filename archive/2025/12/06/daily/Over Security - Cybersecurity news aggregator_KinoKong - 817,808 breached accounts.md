# KinoKong Data Breach

## What Happened

In March 2021, the Russian online streaming service KinoKong suffered a data breach [that was later redistributed as part of a larger corpus of data](https://cybernews.com/security/billions-passwords-credentials-leaked-mother-of-all-breaches/). The breach exposed over 800k unique email addresses along with names, usernames, IP addresses and MD5 password hashes.

## Compromised Data

Email addresses

IP addresses

Names

Passwords

Usernames

## Recommended Actions

##### Change Your Password

If you haven’t already changed the password affected by this breach, do
so immediately on every account where it was used.

##### Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Wherever 2FA is supported, add an extra layer of security to your
account.
