DEF CON Training will be offering intensive, hands-on 2-day trainings with world-class instructors ahead of DEF CON Singapore 2026. Additional classes will be listed as they are approved

Join us at the Marina Bay Sands April 26-27, 2026.

Secure your spots before classes are full! Early bird pricing ends on Feb 8 at 11.59pm (SGT) and late registration pricing starts at 12 am April 5

Please email training@defcon.org for wire payment instructions or to discuss other payment alternatives.
