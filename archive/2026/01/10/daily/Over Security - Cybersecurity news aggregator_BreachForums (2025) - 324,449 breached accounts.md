# BreachForums (2025) Data Breach

## What Happened

In October 2025, [a reincarnation of the hacking forum BreachForums, which had previously been shut down multiple times, was taken offline by a coalition of law enforcement agencies](https://therecord.media/breachforums-fbi-france-takedown-banner-scattered-spider-salesforce-leak). In the months leading up to the takedown, the site itself suffered a data breach that exposed 324k unique email addresses, usernames, and Argon2 password hashes.
