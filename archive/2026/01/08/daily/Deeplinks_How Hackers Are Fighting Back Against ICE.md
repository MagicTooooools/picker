ICE has been invading U.S. cities, targeting, [surveilling](https://www.brennancenter.org/our-work/research-reports/ice-wants-go-after-dissenters-well-immigrants), [harassing](https://www.propublica.org/article/immigration-dhs-american-citizens-arrested-detained-against-will), [assaulting](https://www.opb.org/article/2025/12/06/vancouver-ice-alleged-crushing-legs/), [detaining](https://kstp.com/kstp-news/local-news/woman-says-she-was-detained-by-ice-in-minneapolis-for-being-a-citizen-observer/), and [torturing](https://www.amnesty.org/en/latest/news/2025/12/estados-unidos-nuevas-investigaciones-revelan-violaciones-de-derechos-humanos-en-los-centros-de-detencion-de-alligator-alcatraz-y-krome-en-florida/) people who are undocumented immigrants. They also have [targeted people](https://www.propublica.org/article/immigration-dhs-american-citizens-arrested-detained-against-will) with [work permits](https://www.opb.org/article/2025/10/30/tacoma-hillsboro-victor-cruz-ice-immigration-oregon-law-enforcement/), [asylum seekers](https://truthout.org/articles/ice-kidnapped-my-neighbor-in-broad-daylight-the-aftermath-left-me-reeling/), [permanent residents](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/dec/15/ice-lawsuit-violent-assault) (people holding “green cards”), [naturalized citizens](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/ice-arrest-cedar-riverside-minneapolis-somali-man/), and [even citizens by birth.](https://www.houstonchronicle.com/opinion/outlook/article/i-m-citizen-ice-wrongfully-detained-me-21244415.php) ICE has spent [hundreds of millions of dollars on surveillance technology](http://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/01/ice-going-surveillance-shopping-spree) to spy on anyone—and potentially everyone—in the United States. It can be hard to imagine how to defend oneself against such an overwhelming force. But a few enterprising hackers have started projects to do counter surveillance against ICE, and hopefully protect their communities through clever use of technology.

Let’s start with Flock, the company behind a number of [automated license plate reader](https://sls.eff.org/technologies/automated-license-plate-readers-alprs) (ALPR) and other camera technologies.
