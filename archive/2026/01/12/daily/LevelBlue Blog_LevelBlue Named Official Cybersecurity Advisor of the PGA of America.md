# LevelBlue Named Official Cybersecurity Advisor of the PGA of America

![](https://levelblue.com/hubfs/Blogs/Trustwave_Blog/Headers/PGA-Blog-Header.jpg)

January 12, 2026
2 Minute Read

There is more to running a successful and secure golf facility than making sure the greens are true and sand traps are raked. As golf courses and their associated client-facing amenities become more automated and data-driven, the need for top-flight cybersecurity has become paramount.

To meet this goal, the [PGA of America](https://www.pga.com/), one of the world’s largest sports organizations, has inked a multi-year partnership with LevelBlue to provide [comprehensive cybersecurity services](/services/) to the PGA of America, securing the Association’s digital infrastructure and flagship events. Beyond securing the Association and its major championships, LevelBlue will provide golf courses, facilities, and the thousands of businesses driving the golf economy with access to specialized managed security services and strategic advisory.

PGA of America events include the PGA Championship, the Ryder Cup, and the KPMG Women’s PGA Championship.

“LevelBlue is a recognized leader with unmatched expertise in cybersecurity, and we are proud to join forces,” said Kevin Scott, PGA of America Chief Technology Officer. “While securing our flagship events is a priority, this partnership is about the wider industry. Together with LevelBlue, we are committed to providing the solutions needed to protect golf facilities and businesses across the country.”
