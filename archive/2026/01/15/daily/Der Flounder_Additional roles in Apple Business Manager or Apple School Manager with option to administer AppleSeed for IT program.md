## Additional roles in Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager with option to administer AppleSeed for IT program

January 15, 2026
[rtrouton](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/author/rtrouton/ "Posts by rtrouton") [Leave a comment](#respond)
[Go to comments](#comments)

As a follow-up to my [previous post on using Apple Business Manager to enroll in the AppleSeed for IT program](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/2026/01/04/signing-up-for-appleseed-for-it-using-apple-business-manager/), my colleague [Mark](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mbuff/) let me know that in addition to the [Administrator](https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-business-manager/intro-to-roles-and-privileges-axm97dd59159/web) role, it appears there are two other roles which can administer the [AppleSeed for IT program](https://beta.apple.com/for-it) for an organization.

**Note:** One of those roles is exclusive to [Apple School Manager](https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-school-manager/welcome/web), so for [Apple Business Manager](https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-business-manager/welcome/web) there is only one other role in addition to the **Administrator** role which can administer the AppleSeed for IT program.

* [People Manager](https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-business-manager/intro-to-roles-and-privileges-axm97dd59159/web) role (available in Apple Business Manager and Apple School Manager):

If you look in the **People Manager** role in either Apple Business Manager and Apple School Manager, there is an **Administer AppleSeed for IT** checkbox option.

![](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/screenshot-2026-01-14-at-12.40.png?w=598&h=324 "Screenshot 2026-01-14 at 12.40.png")

This option is disabled by default, but it is the same checkbox option which is checked for the **Administrator** role, which in turn allows the **Administrator** role to administer the AppleSeed for IT program for an organization.

![](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-1.02.png?w=599&h=324 "Screenshot 2026-01-15 at 1.02.png")

* [Site Manager](https://support.apple.com/guide/apple-school-manager/intro-to-roles-and-privileges-axm97dd59159/web) role (available only in Apple School Manager):

If you look in the **Site Manager** role in Apple School Manager, there likewise is an **Administer AppleSeed for IT** checkbox option. This option is enabled by default, so it looks like the **Site Manager** role in Apple School Manager by default has the same ability as the **Administrator** role to administer the AppleSeed for IT program for an organization.

![](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-11.22.49am.png?w=600&h=388 "Screenshot 2026-01-15 at 11.22.49 AM.png")
